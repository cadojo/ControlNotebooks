What's all this?

Welcome to publicly available notes, with working and run-able examples, which provide an overview to control theory. The intended audience are engineering students (undergraduate, early graduate) or new professionals who might want a refresher for fundamental control theory concepts.

We'll walk through simple definitions, motivate controls as a topic, outline linear systems theory and why it is important, and discuss linear and nonlinear controller design and analysis. Throughout these notes, one system will be used as an example: a polynomial model for a model aircraft, developed by NASA.
